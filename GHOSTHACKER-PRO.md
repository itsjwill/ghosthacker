# Ghost Hacker Pro vs Ghost Hacker Lite

## Technical Differences

**Ghost Hacker Pro** is built on advanced, LLM-powered data flow analysis inspired by the ideas of the [LLM-driven Data-Flow Analysis paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.10754). It traces data flows to identify complex, exploitable vulnerabilities with high precision. It's cloud-based with native CI/CD integration (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins) and supports self-hosted deployment.

## Which to Choose?

**Ghost Hacker Lite**: Individual researchers, small teams, or testing personal projects  
**Ghost Hacker Pro**: Designed for organizations that want to "shift-left" and integrate security directly into their development lifecycle. Its _advanced LLM-powered data flow analysis engine_ is ideal for catching deep-seated vulnerabilities before they ever reach production, complemented by full CI/CD integration and enterprise support.
